About Us!

Shopalix is a comprehensive and user-friendly database management website designed specifically for the
employees of our online shopping store. It serves as a centralized platform where employees can
efficiently access and update critical information regarding customers, ordered articles, and shipments.
This secure platform allows authorized personnel to retrieve detailed customer profiles, track orders, manage
inventory, and monitor the status of shipments. With its intuitive interface, employees can seamlessly add,
edit, or remove data, ensuring that the information remains up-to-date and accurate.
